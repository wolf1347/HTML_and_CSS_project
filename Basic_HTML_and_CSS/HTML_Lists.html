Basic requirements for creating a DnD character

  1. Pick your race
  2. Pick your class
  3. Allocate your stat points
  4. Select your feats
  5. Roll your hit die for HP
  6. Allocate your skill points
  7. Select your starter gear
  8. Create your backstory

Basic tools to bring on your first session

  • Character sheet
  • Character Mini
  • Dice set (or 2, or 3)
  • Pencil
  • Paper
